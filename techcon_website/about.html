Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small meetup for local developers in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into an internationally recognized event attracting thousands of attendees from all corners of the tech world.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to foster innovation, share cutting-edge knowledge, and connect individuals passionate about shaping the future of technology. We believe in creating an inclusive and collaborative environment where ideas thrive and technology benefits everyone.

Notable Past Speakers

Jane Smith

Jane Smith is a pioneer in artificial intelligence and machine learning. She spoke at TechCon 2019 about ethical AI and its impact on global industries.

Michael Lee

Michael Lee, a cloud infrastructure expert, captivated audiences in 2021 with his keynote on the future of decentralized computing and open-source technologies.

Sara Gomez

Sara Gomez delivered a powerful session on data privacy and security at TechCon 2022, emphasizing best practices for safeguarding digital assets in the modern world.
